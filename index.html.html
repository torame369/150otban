Delivery Information

Area of Delivery

Phnom Penh area only

Date and Time of Delivery

Monday to Sunday (08:00AM to 05:00PM)

Same Day Delivery

Cut-off time is 03:00PM, orders received after 03:00PM will be delivered on the next day.

How to Place Order

Via E-mail or Phone

If you have any further questions, feel free to contact us at +85512 362 118 or e-mail us at info@dadana.net.

We recommend placing your order 2-3 days before the delivery to ensure availability. If we have any difficulties with your order, we will contact you by phone or email.

Mode of Payment

We accept cash or cash on delivery for ordered products. For engagements, weddings, venue dress-up, event planning, and supply of essentials for all occasions, contact us at +85512 362 118 or info@dadana.net.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation of orders should be done 24hrs before delivery. A 50% cancellation fee will be charged. Orders that have already been prepared and finished cannot be cancelled.
